I have watched a lot of movies, but few movies have stayed with me over the years as these have. Take a look at some of my favorite movies below.

1)Gadar : One of Sunny Deol's best movies, it is the story of a Sikh man who marries a Pakistani woman who crossed the border by mistake. They have a child but they are soon separated as the parents of the girl take her back home. How Tara, the protagonist reunites his family is the crux of this story.

2)Lagaan : Aamir Khan plays Bhuvan, a farmer who lives in a village taxed heavily by the British. Based in pre-independence India, Bhuvan and his friends stake it all on a cricket match between themselves and the British, to waive off all their taxes, or'Lagaan'.

3)Dangal : A father wanted sons so they could carry his legacy as a wrestler, forward. However, he was blessed with daughters. How this family overcame societal taboo to sire multiple champion athletes is the story of Dangal.
